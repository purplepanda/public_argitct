ARGitct, LLC provides a wide range of Strategy, Management + Execution Services related to the architecture, planning, design, real estate, and construction industries.

Whether working on planning a new build-out, untangling an oddly-written contract or a tricky regulatory matter, providing quality-control to a team-member's work, or bringing fresh leadership to a struggling construction project, ARGitct provides the profesional advice, guidance, resources, support, and expertise for Clients to make informed decisions and to proceed forward with confidence and clarity.

Services

  • Strategies and support services for Construction Project Cost Reduction, Acceleration, Recovery, and Re-structuring
  • Strategies and support services for Design + Construction Project Claims, Litigation, and Arbitration matters
  • Contract reviews for Design + Construction Projects
  • Project Representation + Project Advocacy services
  • Project Administration and Close-out services
  • Business Planning + Change Management services
  • Support services for Project Purchasing and Procurement
  • Interim, stop-gap, and holdover services to Designers + Contractors
